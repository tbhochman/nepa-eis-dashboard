About This Dashboard

The NEPA EIS Dashboard tracks the timelines of federal Environmental Impact Statements (EIS) prepared under the National Environmental Policy Act. It covers every stage of the EIS process — from the Notice of Intent (NOI) through the Draft EIS, Final EIS, and Record of Decision (ROD) — providing a comprehensive view of how long these reviews take and how timelines vary by agency, project category, and state.

Data is compiled from the Council on Environmental Quality's EIS Timeline Reports and the EPA's EIS Database.
