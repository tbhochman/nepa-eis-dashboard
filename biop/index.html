Methodology & Data Notes

This dashboard presents 4,694 formal biological opinions issued by the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service under Section 7 of the Endangered Species Act, as recorded in the ECOSPHERE Biological Opinion Report. The data spans 1989–2026 but is most complete from 2006 onward (when the TAILS tracking system became mandatory).

Key limitations: This is USFWS only — NMFS/NOAA Fisheries opinions are tracked separately in the ECO system. Jeopardy/no-jeopardy findings and incidental take statements are not available in the public data. The Malcom & Li (2015) PNAS study found ~975 formal consultations per year from the internal TAILS system, suggesting the public ECOSPHERE report captures only a subset. Annual volume before ~2010 is significantly underrepresented.
