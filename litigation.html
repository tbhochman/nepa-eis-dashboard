Data Sources & Methodology:
Primary litigation data from Navigating NEPA (Breakthrough Institute / Holland & Knight), covering ~1,800 hand-curated NEPA cases from 2012–2022 with full metadata including plaintiffs, agencies, courts, project names, prevailing party, and days in litigation.
Supplemented with federal court opinions from CourtListener (Free Law Project) for 2022–2026 coverage via keyword matching on "National Environmental Policy Act" and related statutory references.
Agency win rate calculated as percentage of cases with a clear NEPA-claim outcome where the agency prevailed. Duration reflects days from challenge filing to court ruling (BTI data only).
This dataset is actively being expanded. Contributions and corrections welcome.
